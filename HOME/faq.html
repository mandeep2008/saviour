Frequently Asked Questions:-

Q: Can I donate only unused medicines?
A: We directly accept medicines from pharmacies, wholesalers and manufactures.If you are indivisuals with unused medicine to donate,you can donate through any of our NGO or executive.You can also contribute to our cause with our monetory donation.Every little bit helps, and we appriciate your support.

Q: I am an indivisual & I want to donate my unused medicine. How?
A: To donate your unused medicine as an indivisual you can be a donator. Follow the procedure in the User Signup to create your account and then, you can get started and be a part of the Donation Process.

Q: I want to request for some medicine.How?
A:Not just for NGOs' but we also understand an indivisual's need for medicine. So, if you wish to request for medicines simply go to the Request tab on our welcome page and there you can request for the medicine you need.

Q: Who are you and what is your platform for?
A: Not taking your medications often leads to worse outcomes with respect to your health. That's why we're here. Our website,Online Unused Medicine Donation System(OUMDS) provide access to medications so that everyone gets the care they deserve.

Q: Can I contribute to the mission being an executive volunteer?
A:Not everyone has medicine to donate. You can join us and become a part of the impact by being an executive or volunteer. Any indivisual interested to be an intermediator between the donator and the recieving end(NGO or an indivisual), can apply for the same.

Q:Where can I get medicine generic name and expiry date?
A:You can get the generic name,expiry date and various other details of medicines from the list of donated medicines by the Donator.

Q:Can I donate expired medicine?
A:Our system is built to ensure proper healthcare of people. Expired medicinesare harmful to the health of any person and donation of the same is strictly prohibited.
